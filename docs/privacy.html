PulsePlan Privacy Policy

Effective date: Apr 27, 2026

PulsePlan is a wellness/fitness app and is not a medical device. It is intended for informational purposes only.

Data we collect

  • No server collection: PulsePlan does not collect or store your personal data on our servers.
  • On-device inputs: The details you enter (age, gender, height, weight, activity level, goal) are used only to calculate the results shown in the app.
  • Heart rate / HRV: In this prototype, the scan values are simulated for demonstration.

Device permissions

PulsePlan does not require access to camera, microphone, contacts, location, SMS, or call logs for the core experience.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Ads & analytics

No ads and no third‑party analytics are used in this version. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

Children’s privacy

VitalScan is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

Because we do not transmit your data to a backend in this version, your entered data stays on your device during use.
